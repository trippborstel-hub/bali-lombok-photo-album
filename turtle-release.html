Baby Turtle Release Day

Green Lion Indonesia • Lombok • April 9, 2026

After days of volunteering with Green Lion Indonesia — cleaning tanks, chopping fish, laying eggs in the hatchery, and caring for dozens of baby sea turtles — it was finally time to set them free.

Staff and volunteers from around the world gathered on the beach in Lombok, each holding a tiny turtle. On the guide's signal, the babies were placed on the sand and began their instinctive crawl toward the ocean. Watch as they scramble across the beach, hit the first waves, and swim out into the open sea.

The Billings Borstel family threw a Turtle Release Lunch Party at their villa afterward to celebrate with the whole Green Lion crew.
